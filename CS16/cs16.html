Counter Strike 1.6



About



Counter-Strike 1.6 was officially released in September 12 2003. It was developed by Valve Corporation, based on a mod originally created by Minh "Gooseman" Le and Jess Cliffe. Counter-Strike started as a fan-made mod for Half-Life in 1999. It quickly became very popular, leading Valve to hire Minh Le and Jess Cliffe and officially bring the project under their company. CS 1.6 became the final major update to the original Counter-Strike series before Valve moved on to Counter-Strike: Source and later games like CS:GO. CS 1.6 is iconic for its simple, skill-based gameplay and is considered a milestone in competitive online shooters.
