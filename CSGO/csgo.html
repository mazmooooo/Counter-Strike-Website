Counter Strike Global Offensive



About



Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) was released on August 21, 2012. It was developed by Valve Corporation and Hidden Path Entertainment. CS:GO is the fourth main game in the Counter-Strike series. It built on the core gameplay — terrorists vs. counter-terrorists — but introduced new weapons, maps, updated graphics, matchmaking systems, and a larger focus on competitive esports. Over time, CS:GO became one of the biggest games in professional gaming history. It also introduced features like skins, cases, and a ranked mode, which massively boosted its popularity. In 2023, it was succeeded by Counter-Strike 2, but CS:GO still holds a legendary spot in gaming culture.
